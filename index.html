About Bayonet Essence

Founded in 2024, Bayonet Essence brings together traditional perfumery craftsmanship with modern sophistication. Each fragrance is carefully curated to tell a unique story.

We believe that perfume is more than just a scent - it's an expression of personality, memory, and emotion. Our master perfumers blend the finest ingredients from around the world to create unforgettable experiences.

Every bottle is crafted with passion, precision, and an unwavering commitment to quality.
